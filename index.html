What is AI?

AI = Artificial Intelligence

AI means computers that can do tasks that usually need human thinking:

  • Learning from experience
  • Understanding language
  • Recognizing patterns
  • Making decisions
  • Solving problems

Important: AI is NOT Human

AI does NOT have:

  • ‚ùå Real understanding (like humans)
  • ‚ùå Feelings or emotions
  • ‚ùå Consciousness (awareness)
  • ‚ùå True creativity

AI finds patterns in data and uses them to give answers.

History of AI - The Beginning

1950: Alan Turing asked "Can machines think?"

1956: The term "Artificial Intelligence" was created at a conference in Dartmouth, USA

1956: First AI program created - it could prove math theorems

Early researchers thought machines would be as smart as humans in 20 years. They were wrong - it was much harder!

Chess-Playing Computers

1997: IBM's Deep Blue beat world champion Garry Kasparov

1951: First chess-playing computer programs (played badly)

1968: A computer beat a human in a tournament for the first time

1997: Deep Blue won against the world's best player!

This showed computers could be better than humans at some thinking tasks.

Types of AI Today

1. Narrow AI (Weak AI) - Does ONE job very well

Examples: Face recognition, Siri/Alexa, Netflix recommendations

2. Machine Learning - Learns from data

The computer finds patterns without being told exactly what to do

3. Deep Learning - Multiple layers of learning

Very good at understanding images, speech, and language

More Types of AI

4. Generative AI - Creates new things

Can write text, create images, make music, write code

Examples: ChatGPT, Claude, DALL-E, Midjourney

5. Computer Vision - Understands pictures and videos

Used in: Medical scans, self-driving cars, factory quality checks

What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI = AI that can work by itself to reach a goal

Regular AI: You ask a question ‚Üí It gives an answer

Agentic AI: You give a goal ‚Üí It makes a plan ‚Üí It takes many steps ‚Üí It reaches the goal

Example: You say "Research electric cars and write a report." The AI will search information, read websites, organize data, and write the report - all by itself!
